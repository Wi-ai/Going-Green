How to make Rehmannia Root Tea, Chicken Soup, Rahmannia Wine

Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 slices dried rehmannia root(or about 5-10 grams)
  • 2 cups water
  • Optional: goji berries, red dates,honey
Steps:
  • Rinse the rehmannia root briefly.
  • Add it to a pot with 2 cup water.
  • Bring to a boil, then simmer 20-30 minutes.
  • Strain and drink as a warming tea.
  • Optional: Add goji berries or red dates during simmering for extra.
  • Sweetness

Chinese Herbal Chicken Soup with Rehmannia

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken thighs or a small chicken
  • 3-5 slices dried rehmannia root
  • 5-8 dried red dates
  • 1 tbsp goji berries
  • 1-2 slices ginger
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  • Place chicken in a pot, add water to cover.
  • Bring to boil, skim off foam.
  • Add rehmannia root, dates,goji berries, and ginger.
  • simmer for 1-2 hours over low heat.
  • Season with a little salt and enjoy this nourishing, slightly sweet herbal.

Rehmannia Wine

Ingredients:

  • 50g dried rehmannia root
  • 500ml rice wine(or vodka)
Steps:
  • Place rehmannia root in a clean glass jar.
  • Pour rice wine over to completely.
  • Seal and let sit in a cool, dark place for 2-4 weeks, shaking occasionally
  • Strain and store.
  • drink small amounts(1-2 tbsp/day) as a tonic.
Note:
  • Rehmannia can be very warming and nourishing but may not suit everyone (e.g., people with weak digestion or damp conditions)
  • Always use moderate amounts and consult with a practitioner if using for health conditions
