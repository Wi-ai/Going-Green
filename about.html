Who We Are?

Going Green is an inovative health app that promoted plants rather than drugs. It gives Knowledge to users about plants

How it Works?

When you go to the App, you chose and talk to a doctor about your condition, the doctor tells you of a plant to use then you go and search for the plant in the home page. If you want you can look at the components of the plant, what to add to meet your needs or you just order it from the app. The app will suggest plant shops where you can get that plant and you can talk to the shop for more information. After getting the plant and starting the treatment, your doctor will be notified every week of your improvement using the photoplethysmography method to check. If your condition is different, you can set an appointment with the doctor from the app.
