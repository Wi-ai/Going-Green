How to make Goji Berry Tea, Rice Porridge, Salad Topper, energy balls and Chicken Soup

Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 tbsp dried goji berries
  • 2 cups for hot water
  • Optional: chrysanthemum flowers, honey, lemon
Steps:
  • Place goji berries in a teapot or cup.
  • Pour over hot water(not boiling) and steep 5-10 minutes.
  • Drink the tea.
  • Don't waaste the plumped-up berries, eat them too.

Goji Berry Rice Porridge(Congee)

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup rice
  • 4-5 cups water or broth
  • 2-3 tbsp dried goji berries
  • Optional: red dates, ginger, a drizzle of honey or sprinkle of salt
Steps:
  • Simmer rice in water/broth until soft and porridge-like(45-60 minutes).
  • Add goji berries and continue simmering for 10 more minutes.
  • Serve warm.

Goji Berry Salad Topper

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Handful of dried goji berries(soaked if you prefer soft)
  • Nuts or seeds(like almonds or sunflower seeds)
  • Feta or goat cheese(optional)
  • Simple vinaigrette
Steps:
  • Toss greens with toppings.
  • Drizzle with vinaigrette.
  • Enjoy the pops of sweetness from the goji

Goji Berry Energy Balls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oats
  • ½ cup nut butter
  • ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
  • ¼ cup dried goji berries
  • Optional: chia seeds, cocoa powder
Steps:
  • Mix everything in a bowl.
  • Roll into small balls.
  • Chill in fridge for 30 minutes
  • Eat as a quick, healthy snack

Goji Berry Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • 1 tbsp dried goji berries
  • 4-5 dried red dates
  • Ginger slices
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  • Simmer chicken with dates and ginger for 1-2 hours.
  • Add goji berries in last 10-15 minutes.
  • Season and enjoy this nourishing Chinese-style soup
