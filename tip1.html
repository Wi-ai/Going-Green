How to make Lavender Sachet/Tea

Lavender Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon dried lavender buds(culinary grade)
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • Optional: honey, lemon, chamomile, or mint
Steps:
  • Boil 1 cup of water.
  • Place the dried lavender buds in a tea infuser or directly into your mug.
  • Pour the boiling water over the lavender.
  • Cover and let steep for 5–7 minutes (lavender can be strong, so taste after
  • Strain if needed and pour into your cup.
  • Sweeten with honey or add a bit of lemon if you like.
  • Sip slowly and enjoy!

Lavender Sachet (for Relaxation)

You’ll need:

  • ½ cup dried lavender buds
  • Small fabric squares (cotton or muslin, about 4x4 inches) or small drawstring bags
  • Ribbon or string (if using fabric squares)
  • Optional: a few dried rose petals or chamomile for extra scent
Steps:
  • Place 2–3 tablespoons of dried lavender buds in the center of the fabric square (or directly in the drawstring bag).
  • If you want, mix in a small pinch of dried rose petals or chamomile for a floral boost.
  • Gather the corners of the fabric together and tie securely with ribbon or string.
  • Give the sachet a gentle squeeze to release the oils and scent.
How to Use:
  • Tuck into your pillowcase for calming sleep.
  • Place in a drawer, closet, or handbag for a fresh scent
  • Keep one by your work desk and squeeze when you need a little stress relief.
