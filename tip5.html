How to make Mugwort Herbal Tea

Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp dried mugwort(Artemisia Vulgaris)
  • 1 cup hot water
  • Optional: honey, lemon
Steps:
  • Boil water and let it cool slightly.
  • Place mugwort in a teapot or cup.
  • Pour hot water over the herbs.
  • Steep for 5-100 minutes(shorter for mild, longer for stronger flavor).
  • Strain, add honey or lemon if you like, and sip!.
Note
  • Mugwort is very potent don't overconsume(max 1-2 small cups/dy).

Korean Mugwort Rice Cakes(Ssuk Tteok)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour
  • 2-3 tbsp mugwort powder(or finely chopped fresh mugwort)
  • 2–3 tbsp sugar
  • Water, as needed
Steps:
  • mix rice flour, mugwort, sugar, and a little water to form a dough.
  • Shape into small balls or press into a mold.
  • Steam for 10-15 minutes until soft an chewy.
  • Cool slightly before eating.
  • Then enjoy the subtle earthy flavor!.

Mugwort Omelette or fritters

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 tbsp fresh chopped mugwort(or 1 tsp dried)
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt, pepper
  • Optional: green onions, cheese
Steps:
  • Beat the eggs, add mugwort, salt,and pepper.
  • Heat a little oil in a pan, pour in the egg mixture.
  • Cook like a regular omelette or make small fritters.
  • serve hot

Mugwort Bath(relaxing)

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 cups dried mugwort
  • A pot of boiling water
steps:
  • Steep mugwort in boiling water for 15-20 minutes.
  • Strain and pour the herbal water into your bath.
  • Soak and relax.
Uses:
  • This bath is believed to ease tension and muscle aches.
