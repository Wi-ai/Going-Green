How to make Ginseng Tea

Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 dried ginseng root slices(or 1 tsp dried ginseng powder)
  • 2 cups water
  • Optional: honey, lemon, fresh ginger
Steps:
  • Bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  • Add the ginseng slices or powder.
  • Reduce heat and simmer gently for 10-20 minutes(the longer, the stronger the tea).
  • Strain into a cup.
  • Add honey or lemon if you like.
  • Enjoy
Note
  • You can reuse ginseng slices 2-3 times.

Ginseng Honey Lemon Energy Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 ginseng root slices
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1–2 tsp fresh lemon juice
Steps:
  • Place ginseng slices in cup.
  • pour boiling water over them.
  • Let steep for 10-15 minutes(cover the cup to keep the steam in).
  • Remove slices, stir in honey and lemon.
  • sip slowly.
Note:
  • This is hret for energy nd focus

Ginseng Green Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp green tea leaves(or 1 teabag)
  • 1 ginseng root slice
  • 1 cup boiling water
Steps:
  • Steep the ginseng slice in hot water forr 10 minutes.
  • Add green tea leaves or bag and steep for 2-3 minutes more.
  • Strain nd drink
  • Strain the liquid, discard petals and spices.
  • Let the syrup cool and store in a glass bottle/jar in the fridge.
