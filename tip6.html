How to cook and make natural medicine/Tea

Schisandra berry Herbal Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 tbsp dried schisandra berries
  • 2-3 cups water
  • Optional: honey or a slice of ginger
Steps:
  • Add berries and water to a pot.
  • Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer 15-20 minutes.
  • Strain into a cup.
  • Sweeten with honey or add ginger if you like!.
  • You can reuse the berries 1-2 more times

Schisandra Berry Cold Infusion

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp dried Schisandra berries
  • 1-2 cups cold water
  • Optional: lemon slice, mint
Steps:
  • Place berries in a glass or jar.
  • Cover with cold.
  • let infuse in the fridge for 4-6 hour(or overnight)
  • Strain and drink chilled

Schisandra Berryy Honey Syrup

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup dried Schisandra berries.
  • 1 cup water.
  • 1 cup honey
Steps:
  • Simmer berries in water for 20-30 minutes.
  • strain and let the liquid cool
  • Mix the cooledd schisandr tea with honey
  • Store in a jar
  • You can use 1 tbsp in tea,drizzle over yogurt, or mix into cocktils!

Schisandra Berry Elixir or Tonic

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp schisandra berry syrup(or strong tea).
  • Sparkling water
  • Ice + lemon wedge
steps:
  • Fill a glass with ice.
  • Add schisandra syrup or tea
  • Top with sparkling water and a squeeze of lemon
  • Stir and sip
